## About This Certification

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate validates the ability to design and implement distributed systems on Amazon Web Services. The exam covers core AWS services, architectural best practices, and cost optimization strategies.

## Topics Covered

- Designing resilient, high-performing architectures
- Cost-optimized and secure architectures on AWS
- Compute, storage, database, and networking services
- IAM policies, encryption, and compliance frameworks
- Multi-region deployment strategies and disaster recovery
