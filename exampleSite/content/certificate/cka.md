## About This Certification

The Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) program provides assurance that CKAs have the skills, knowledge, and competency to perform the responsibilities of Kubernetes administrators.

## Topics Covered

- Cluster architecture, installation, and configuration
- Workload scheduling and lifecycle management
- Services, networking, and ingress controllers
- Storage provisioning and persistent volumes
- Troubleshooting clusters and application failures
